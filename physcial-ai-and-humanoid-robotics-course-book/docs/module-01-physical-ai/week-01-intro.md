## What is Embodied Intelligence?

Embodied intelligence refers to cognitive processes that are deeply rooted in the body's interactions with the world. Unlike traditional AI, which relies purely on computation, embodied AI combines perception, action, and physical presence.

### Key Concepts

1. **Perception**: Robots sense their environment through sensors (cameras, LIDAR, IMU)
2. **Cognition**: The robot processes this sensory data to understand and decide
3. **Action**: The robot acts in the world based on its decisions
4. **Learning**: The robot learns from the feedback loop of sense-think-act

## Why Physical Robotics?

Physical robots differ from simulations because they:
- Must deal with real-world uncertainties and noise
- Have physical constraints (power, size, speed)
- Can interact with humans and physical objects
- Learn through embodied experience
